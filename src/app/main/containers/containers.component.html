Overview

According to layout of the container (row or column) the children could be distributed using the fxLayoutAlign directive.

The fxLayoutAlign directive takes two words. The first word says how the children will be aligned in the layout's direction, and the second word says how the children will be aligned perpendicular to the layout's direction.
